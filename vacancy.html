Job Vacancy

Are you looking for a fun part-time job?

Are you looking for a fun part-time job or holiday job in a friendly and international environment? Then we are looking for you!

What will you do?

  • Stocking shelves and presenting products attractively;
  • Helping customers with their questions;
  • Operating the checkout;
  • Checking best-before dates;
  • Assisting with receiving and processing deliveries;
  • Keeping the store clean and tidy.

What do we ask?

  • You are enthusiastic and customer-friendly;
  • You can work independently but are also a great team player;
  • You are flexible and available for evenings and weekends;
  • No experience is required — we are happy to train you;
  • You speak Dutch or English.

What do we offer?

  • A varied part-time job in an international working environment;
  • Flexible working hours that fit well around school or studies;
  • A competitive salary;
  • A friendly team with colleagues from different nationalities;
  • The opportunity to gain valuable retail experience.

Who is this for?

This position is ideal as a part-time job alongside school or studies, but it is also very suitable as a holiday job during school breaks.

Does this sound like something you would enjoy? Then apply today!

Internship

Internship at Our International Supermarket

Are you looking for a valuable and varied internship in the retail sector? Then you’ve come to the right place.

What will you learn during your internship?

  • Customer-friendly and service-oriented work;
  • Stocking and presenting products;
  • Inventory management and logistical processes;
  • Checkout operations;
  • Working according to hygiene and safety guidelines;
  • Collaborating in a dynamic retail environment.

What do we offer?

  • Personal guidance from experienced colleagues;
  • A safe and professional learning environment;
  • The opportunity to gain practical experience in an international workplace;
  • Support to achieve your educational learning goals;
  • A varied internship where you actively contribute to the team.

We believe it’s important that interns can develop themselves and build professional skills with confidence. That’s why we provide good supervision, clear agreements, and plenty of room to learn and ask questions.

Are you motivated, eager to learn, and looking for a challenging internship? Feel free to contact us to discuss the possibilities. We look forward to welcoming you to our team!
