Our Story

As of January 1, 2025, we, Dilan and Baris, are proud to have taken over our family’s international supermarket. For 25 years, our father ran the supermarket with great dedication and passion, building a strong reputation within the community. With deep respect for his work, we are continuing this tradition while adding new ideas and a fresh vision.

We combine different backgrounds and areas of expertise. Dilan completed a law degree and brings legal knowledge, structure, and entrepreneurship to the business. Baris has completed two master’s degrees in Economics and possesses extensive knowledge in business management, finance, and strategic development. In addition, Baris grew up in Paris, which has given him an international perspective and multicultural experience.

As entrepreneurs with a Kurdish background, we place great importance on hospitality, quality, and connection. Our supermarket is a place where people from different cultures come together and discover products from all over the world. We strive to provide our customers every day with a wide assortment, excellent service, and a familiar, welcoming atmosphere.

With pride, we continue to build on the family business and look forward to the future, where tradition, quality, and innovation go hand in hand.
